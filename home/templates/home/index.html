About Us

Founded in 2010, we are based at Camden Ice Rink where curlers of all ages, backgrounds and abilities are always welcome.

Activities

The curling season runs from October to March and during this time we hold practice sessions every Tuesday and Thursday evening, with league games taking place every Sunday.
